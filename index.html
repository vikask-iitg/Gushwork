What are the benefits of using aluminum tubing in manufacturing?

Aluminum tubing offers light weight, corrosion resistance, excellent machinability, and strong structural performance across industrial applications.

How is aluminum tubing produced?

It is typically extruded or drawn to achieve consistent wall thickness, controlled tolerances, and a smooth surface finish for fabrication.

What are the common applications of aluminum tubing?

Common uses include transportation, infrastructure, machinery frames, packaging systems, and process engineering environments.

Can aluminum tubing be customized?
